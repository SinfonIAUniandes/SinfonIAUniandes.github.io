Sinfonia Uniandes

We are a team of researchers and engineers working on the cutting edge of AI and robotics technology. Our goal is to create intelligent, autonomous robots that can operate in a wide range of environments and perform a variety of tasks.

Our Research

Our research focuses on developing advanced AI algorithms and systems that enable robots to learn from their environment, adapt to new situations, and make decisions on their own. We are also working on new hardware and control systems that enable robots to move and interact with their environment in a more natural and intelligent way.
