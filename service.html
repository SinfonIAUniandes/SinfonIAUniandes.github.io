Our Team

Who are we?

SinfonIA alliance is an initiative from industry and academia to support the application and development of artificial intelligence and robotics in research projects. This alliance is made up of the Bancolombia Artificial Intelligence Competence Center, the Universidad de los Andes, the Santo Tomás University and the University of Magdalena.

Equity

Each member of SinfonIa will have the same job opportunities and projection within the framework of strengthening their investigative skills in didactic and disciplinary matters.

Solidarity:

The members of the seedbed through the different dynamics will develop activities that promote collaboration, cooperation and teamwork with a common interest in training.

Ethical conduct:

The members of the seedbed act with moral integrity, physical integrity, honesty, respect for ideas and loyalty.
